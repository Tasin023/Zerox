🏢 Who We Are

Founded in 2020, NexGen started as a small tech reseller in Dhaka and has grown into a full-scale e-commerce platform trusted by thousands of customers across Bangladesh. We specialize in genuine, warrantied products from top global brands including Apple, Samsung, Logitech, ASUS, and more.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to quality technology at fair prices. Our mission is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge global tech and everyday Bangladeshi consumers — with transparent pricing, honest reviews, and dependable after-sales service you can actually count on.
